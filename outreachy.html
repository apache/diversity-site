Outreachy @ Apache

Apache participates with Outreachy to provide mentorship to people in three month internships to work remotely on projects at the Apache Software Foundation. Outreachy internship projects may include programming, user experience, documentation, illustration, graphical design, or data science.

Outreachy provides paid internships to people around the world to work with free and open source software projects and mentors. Interns work remotely and also get a stipend to attend related conferences and events. Outreachy invites anyone who faces under-representation, systemic bias, or discrimination in the technology industry of their country to apply.
